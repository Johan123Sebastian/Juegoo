Learn City Vocabulary

Welcome to the city! Here you will learn essential vocabulary related to urban life.

Transportation

  • Bus: A large vehicle that transports passengers around the city.
  • Taxi: A car that carries passengers for a fare.
  • Subway: An underground transportation system that is fast and efficient.
  • Bicycle: A two-wheeled vehicle powered by pedaling.
  • Motorcycle: A two-wheeled motorized vehicle, ideal for traffic.
  • Train: A rail-based transport used for long distances within the city.
  • Tram: A vehicle that moves on tracks within the city.
  • Truck: A large vehicle used for transporting goods.
  • Scooter: A small vehicle, motorized or not, for short distances.
  • Ambulance: A medical emergency vehicle that transports patients.

Places

  • Hospital: A place where medical care is provided for sick people.
  • Park: An outdoor space with greenery and recreational areas.
  • School: An institution where children and young people receive education.
  • Restaurant: A place where people go to eat and drink.
  • Library: A place where books can be borrowed and read.
  • Museum: A space where historical and artistic objects are exhibited.
  • Theater: A building where plays and live shows are performed.
  • Stadium: A large venue for sports events and concerts.
  • Bank: A financial institution where people manage their money.
  • Mall: A place with many shops and restaurants for shopping.

People and Professions

  • Doctor: A person who practices medicine and cares for health.
  • Firefighter: A person who extinguishes fires and rescues people in danger.
  • Police Officer: An agent responsible for maintaining order and safety.
  • Teacher: A person who teaches in a school or educational institution.
  • Chef: A person who prepares meals in a restaurant.
  • Architect: A professional who designs buildings and structures.
  • Lawyer: A person who works on legal matters and defends clients.
  • Salesperson: A person who offers products or services to customers.
  • Taxi Driver: A driver of a vehicle that transports passengers.
  • Nurse: A professional who helps doctors take care of patients.
